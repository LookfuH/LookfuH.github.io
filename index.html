CrossDrop

In this click-and-drag letter swapping puzzle, your goal is to arrange all letters in the grid correctly. Yellow letters indicate a letter is in the correct row or column but not the exact position, while green letters are in the correct position. Strategically swap letters to turn them all green, starting with those already green and focusing on yellow letters to guide your moves.
